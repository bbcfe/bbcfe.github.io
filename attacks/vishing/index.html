Vishing

What is Vishing

Vishing attacks are conducted by making calls or leaving voice messages claiming to be part of a reputable organization (eg. banks, government department, etc.). The attacker attempts to convince the victim into providing sensitive personal information such as bank details or payment information.

How can you Identify a Vishing attack?

Often attackers may be aware of only a certain amount of your information. Asking for confirmation of your full name, account number or other personally identifiable information that the company they claim to represent should have can be a good way to identify a scam.
Additionally Vishers will often create a sense of urgency in order to put pressure on the victim into making poor decisions due to being distracted/not taking time to think the situation through.

How to protect yourself

If you aren't familiar with a number that is calling then always be cautious when answering the phone. Be aware of the tactics that a Vishing attack might use so that you can avoid them if they happen.
